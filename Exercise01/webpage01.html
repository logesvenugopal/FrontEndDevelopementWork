Animal Planet

Lion

King of Forest

Lions have strong, compact bodies and powerful forelegs,teeth and jaws for pulling down and killing prey. Their coats are yellow-gold, and adult males have shaggy manes that range in color from blond to reddish-brown to black.

Tiger

Strongest Animal

Tigers are some of the most amazing creatures on the planet, they are the largest members of the cat family and are renowned for their power and strength. As the largest member of the cat family, Tigers are strong, powerful and one of nature's most feared predators.

Elephant

Biggest Animal

Elephants are the largest land mammals on earth and have distinctly massive bodies, large ears, and long trunks. They use their trunks to pick up objects, trumpet warnings, greet other elephants, or suck up water for drinking or bathing, among other uses.

Birds Planet

Parrot

Able to Speak

Parrots are colorful birds with four toes on each foot and hooked beaks, and some are known to copy noises and the way humans talk.

Eagle

Strongest Bird

Eagles are large birds of prey, meaning that they hunt and eat animals for food. They are related to hawks, falcons, and vultures. Eagles have long held a special place in the human imagination because of their strength and soaring flight.

Crow

Smartest One

Crows are large birds with shiny black feathers. They often live together in large families. They are known for their loud voices and their intelligence. These clever, curious birds have a reputation as thieves and pranksters.
